Our Story

More Than a Venue —
It's Family

Rose Valley Outdoor Venue is a family-owned rustic event space nestled in Humble, Texas. Our 1.25-acre property is fully dedicated to your event and designed to make every celebration feel magical, intimate, and unforgettable.

From the moment you arrive, you'll feel the warmth and care that sets us apart. We go above and beyond for every client because at Rose Valley, you're not just a booking — you're family.
